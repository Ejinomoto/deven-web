Application Features

Health Screening

Just use our Health Screening feature, then wear our Health Smartwatch for 7 days x 24 Hours!

Deven will monitor & analyze your Vital Signs and Resting Heart Rate to detect your potential signs of Heart Disease and Stroke, whether Potential Signs are not found, Potential Signs are found, Symptoms and Signs are found If Unhealthy Report is received, Contact Doctor and follow our Preventive solution

Teleconsultation

Consult With Our Doctor Wherever You Are You can consult with our doctor and specialist to discuss your finding, provide preventive solution, and provide curative solution, if necessary.

Wellness, Body Composition and Sport Monitoring

We monitor Wellness Status Based on Early Warning Score For your Health Reminder. The health data taken from the Health Smartwatch or manual Input. We also monitoring your Body Composition score, your sport activities, etc, so that we can suggest improvable Prevention program for your health. Your prevention will start from your Body Composition Score, that the progress can be check every month.

Sport Monitoring

Maintain your health during sport activity, Deven provide recommended Heart Rate so that you can avoid sudden Heart Attack or Stroke. We provide many type of exercise such as Ru, Walk, Cycling, Ping Pong, etc. Please avoid Water Sport as the smartwatch is not designed for Water Sport.

Health Tracker

To easily provide your health update, Deven are connected to Cexme - Health Smartwach. In future, Samsung, Garmin, Huawei, etc will be connected as well. And to improve your Heathy lifestyle, Deven will be directly connected to Body Analyser Scale to report your BMI, Body Fat, Body Water, etc

Electronic Medical Record

Our Health Smartwatch provide continuous vital sign monitoring on hourly basis such as : Blood Pressure, Heart Rate, Temperature, Blood Oxygen, Respiratory rate and ECG. All data are recorded on your medical record, so you can report to your doctor easily. And to complete it, you can add your own data such as Rontgen, Lab Test, Medicine, USG, Genome, and notes.
