About Deven

Deven is an application for detecting potential Stroke, Heart Diseases, and the relating diseases such as Hypertension, Diabetes, Obesity, etc, via Questionnaire and wearing our Cexme - Health Smartwatch for 7 days x 24 hours.

After receiving the analysis & recommendation, please follow the Prevention Solution such as Sport & Calories Burning, Good Sleep, Meal Plan, etc. Our application was powered by Cexup, and based on our research with some hospitals to more than 300 people that found the signs of potential Heart Diseases or Stroke, which then, our method has been reviewed and received award as the 1st Winner by MIT Hacking Medicine, August 2023.
